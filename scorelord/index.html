Welcome to the Site

Here you will find all the information about SCORELORD, as well as my partner channels (Mash-Up Piano and my personal channel). You can read up on the inspiration that went into each of my works and get the latest news on my upcoming projects. Easily access all my content across YouTube and musicnotes.

Inspired By Music You Love

Check out my originals like Veil Waters, which is inspired by the renowned Hollow Knight soundtrack. My inspired, not arranged, works are fresh but yet familiar to the music you know and love.

I Arrange

Find iconic melodies as found in Zelda, Star Wars or Marvel, woven together in new and exciting ways. Find new your favorite scores reimagined in fun, epic, and peculiar ways.

I Compose

Find originals like my Vast Ventures, that bring something new to an existing genre. With no directly inspired or arranged material, this music is open to your imagination and creativity.

Requests

Much of my music comes from requests people give me! While there is certainly music I strive to create, I also put priority in trying new things. Leave a comment on one of my videos, or message me directly and I would be happy to consider your request!
