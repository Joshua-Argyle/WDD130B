My name is Josh Argyle, a contemporary composer for both orchestra and piano. With a love for the works of past composers such as Beethoven, Dvorak, and Wagner, to the modern film scores of John Williams, Hans Zimmer, and Ludwig Goransson, I have grown up with a love for a great deal of different kinds of music. One of the greatest goals of my music is to represent various styles and genres in a singular work. My works cover modern, neoclassical, and even jazz styles. If you want to listen to a variety of music, this is the place!

As a kid I was always fascinated by the scores in film, often tinkering on the piano what I heard from a movie I watched the night before, even though I was supposed to be practicing my scales. While at the time my piano teacher would find my tinkering as somewhat of an annoyance and hinderer to my growth, it later became my passion as I became a more knowledgeable musician. I now see that tinkering as my drive to compose!
